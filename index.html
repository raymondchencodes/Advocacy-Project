Chicago is the best city in the world. But like any major city, it faces its own set of challenges. One notable issue is Chicago's severe illiteracy epidemic.

About My Topic

Chicago's illiteracy rate is one of the highest in the United States, with a high of over 90 percent. This is due to the high population of Chicagoans, who are primarily from low-income backgrounds. According to the 2023 Chicago Public Schools state report card, 12.2% of low-income third graders were reading at the expected grade level.
